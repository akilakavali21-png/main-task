About Our Institution

Discover the Legacy of Excellence in Engineering Education

Our History

National Institute of Technology was established in 1955 as a premier engineering institution. Starting with just three departments and 150 students, we have expanded to five major engineering departments with over 2500 students.

Our alumni network of more than 50,000 engineers serves in leading positions across the globe.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation.

Our Mission

To provide quality engineering education through comprehensive curriculum, state-of-the-art facilities, and experienced faculty.
